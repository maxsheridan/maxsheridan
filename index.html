About

Max Sheridan is the weirdo who wrote the novels Dillo and God's Speedboat, and the Pushcart unshortlisted classics A Penis Boot, Mud Hustler, and I Went to Work at IKEA After Bobby Got Killed at the Bowling Alley. Find his latest tale, Bobby Two Winds, in Pulp Queer Westerns.

Tug Wells

Tug Wells dropped out of the California Career Institute in 2002 to oversee the family mining concern in Paramaribo. After a fishing accident in French Guiana left him tone deaf, he took up graphic design. Conceptual artist Ed Ruscha once described Wells' collages and digital found art. Wells' most recent work can be found in Vogue Detroit, Der Spiegel, Be-Mag, and the New York Times Crossword Puzzle.
